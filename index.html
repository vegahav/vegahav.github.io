About me

Bio

My name is Hector Vega and I'm an aspiring software developer based in Northern Virginia where I have lived most of my life. Coming from humble beginnings as the son of two immigrants, I learned the value of hard work, dedication, and gratitude from an early age.

Growing up I did not have a lot of exposure to technology due to financial constraints. However, I was always fascinated by technologies of all sorts and I considered myself very fortunate when I was finally be able to immerse myself in it. It was then that I knew I had found my true passion.

I pursued my education at James Madison University, where I earned a Bachelor of Science in 2020 in Communication Studies, with a minor in Computer Information Systems. This allowed me to cultivate my technical skills and combine them with my ability to effectively communicate and collaborate with others.

Fast forward to today and I am 3 years into working for AWS which has been an incredibly rewarding experience, allowing me to contribute to the critical systems that power businesses and organizations around the globe. Because I thrive in a dynamic environment, I am constantly seeking opportunities to enhance my technical skills and deepen my understanding with various technologies.

Thank you again for visiting my portfolio website and I hope my work exceeds your expectations. Please do not hesitate to reach out regarding any questions or inclinations to work together. I look forward to hearing from you.
